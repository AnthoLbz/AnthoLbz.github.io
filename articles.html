You too become Iron Man… for 10,000 dollars!

This video features English cosplayer Paul MacDowell during MCM ComicCon in London last May. He didn’t fully design his armor. It comes from a Hong Kong company called KillerBody, which we talked about a few years ago. They designed two models. The one seen in the video is the most advanced since everything is controllable, not just the helmet. This armor is therefore capable of electrically controlling the helmet, the shoulders, the wings, the right forearm and the thighs. All these elements are lit by LED. They even pushed the detail to sound the Arc reactor and the right palm, which does not have a real laser unlike that made by an amateur. To control the whole, a remote control is provided, but it is mainly the magnetic sensors in the fingers that make everything believable. It is therefore possible for everyone to become an Iron Man. Of course, this armor comes at a cost. The KillerBody website notes the 2 models out of stock, but the company has confirmed that they are available again. It will therefore cost you a whopping 4,500 or 10,000 dollars depending on the model. It takes indeed count the 8,999 dollars of the armor to which are added 480 dollars of shipping costs and of course the customs fees. According to the company, it takes 3 to 5 days of delivery to France with DHL, so it's too late for Halloween, especially since this armor is one size and it will therefore be necessary to make alterations in order to best adapt it to your body size.
